GPhoto Uploader.

Usage:
    gphoto-uploader upload --credentials=FILE --photo-list=FILE [--verbose]
    gphoto-uploader (-h | --help)
    gphoto-uploader --version

Options:
    --photo-list=FILE     New line delimited list of image or video files to upload.
    --credentials=FILE    Path to client-secrets.json file.
    -h --help             Show this screen.
    --version             Show version.
    --verbose             Enable verbose logging.
